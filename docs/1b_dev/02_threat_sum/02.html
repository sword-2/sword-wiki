Language considerations

The comparison may be poor, but separation of software groups can be compared to separation of denominations (C++/Java/swift - Baptist/Methodist/independent). One of the SWORD engines was already in Java, a language the information sheet encouraged. There is no intent to further divide, but one individual coming out of C/C++ may observe the Swift language was newer - if they had to learn a new language, they went for the most recent. This does nothing to prevent software separation, but it just a process. Also, Goal a4-6 was added to identify there is possible language (perl) from a believer. The status of that language is not currently known.
