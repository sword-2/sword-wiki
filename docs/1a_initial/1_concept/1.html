Goals

Tables a1-a4 capture some simple goals. The name for each goal group/table are a1 - scripture distribution, a2 - teaching aids, a3 - church business, and a4 - stewardship. The majority of a3/church business is mostly more applications for modules even if the module is just a simple generic book (genbook).

Columns were added that sometimes identify what may be considered unique or partly unique with footnotes at the end of each table. Individual contributors may have varying goals which might have been missed.

a1-1 *1 distribute scripture to as many people within a domain as possible-electronic Existing Users and operators should be able to study the bible without making compromises. That includes not having to compromise by using a platform other than their favorite.
